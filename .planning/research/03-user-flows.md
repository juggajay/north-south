# Research: User Flows & UX Patterns

## Investigation Focus

Detailed user flows for the mobile-first joinery configurator, optimized for thumb-zone navigation and conversion.

---

## 1. Primary Persona: Sarah

### Profile

- **Name:** Sarah, 38
- **Location:** Marrickville, Sydney (Inner West)
- **Housing:** Terrace home, owner-occupier
- **Household:** Dual-income, $130k+ combined
- **Tech comfort:** High (uses apps daily, comfortable with online purchasing)
- **Current state:** Unrenovated alcove under stairs, wants butler's pantry
- **Research done:** Pinterest board with 50+ saved images, got Kinsman quote ($28k, 16 weeks)
- **Pain points:** Too expensive, too slow, can't visualize outcome

### Sarah's Goals

1. See what her actual space could look like transformed
2. Understand pricing without lengthy consultation process
3. Get quality outcome without premium custom joinery price
4. Move fast (not wait 16+ weeks)

### Sarah's Fears

1. Ordering wrong size / measurements being off
2. Quality being disappointing (Kaboodle horror stories)
3. Hidden costs appearing after commitment
4. Communication going silent after payment
